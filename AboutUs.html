About Us

I‘m JT, I have been fishing for about 20 years now. I first started fishing when i was 4 or 5 years old, it all started one morning when i was flipping through channels on the TV looking for Disney but landed on the outdoors channel and watched a bass tournament until my dad came out and found me, i asked “can we do that!”

When we first started out we had no clue what we were doing, but it was always a great time with my dad, and later with my brother Aswell. Growing up we caught a number of different fish from bass trout and walleye to catfish sunfish and perch. After some years fishing, we got a canoe, that opened many doors previously closed as we no longer needed to find a good spot on the shore, all we needed was a public boat launch. Nowadays I mostly fish with my youngest Brother Colin who is in middle school. We fish from Shore and mostly Target Bass and Pickrel. Sometimes we fish at our grandfathers house on the Delaware, that is always a good time.

Why I'm Making This Site

I am creating this website because through many google searches for fishing in the local area the results were underwhelming to say the least. I want to create a resource for Local fisherman to gain tips and tricks, as well as possibly learn of some new public fishing spots. I am also using this website to practice my website development skills and improve them. I will be making Colin write some Articles for you guys to read as well so he can practice his writing skills. If anyone else would like to contribute content reach out to me!
