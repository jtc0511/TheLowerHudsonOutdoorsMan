Beginers guide to getting started fishing

While I'm slightly older and smarter now, in Highschool id often fish the Wallkill River in Middletown NY by wearing a swimsuit and water shoes and wadding down river from the public access point to the Bridge. Id fish the way down and the way back up as well. This was successful for catching 8 – 12 inch smallmouths. Almost every single time I'd go in the Moring, I would catch 5-15 fish in 2-3 hours. I never caught any real gigantic fish in this spot, the largest was around 16 inches long, weighing 2-3 lbs. 14–16-inch fish were common, but it was not a guarantee to catch them ... Read more
