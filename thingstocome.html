The goal of this is to help all anglers, but especially younger or inexperienced anglers located in or around region 3 in New York. I want to provide 1 or 2 posts a month and give insight into the fishing spots I go. Alot of anglers keep their cards close to their chest when it comes to fishing spots, and there is definitely some spots I'm hesitant to share with you all, but I do not want to be a gate keeper and I want to help the fishing community grow and flourish.
